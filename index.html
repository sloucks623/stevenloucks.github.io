My Journey into Cybersecurity

After 20+ years operating tower cranes, managing Domino’s stores, and running a high-demand luxury auto transport business, I’ve transitioned into cybersecurity. My work has always required precision, responsibility, and problem-solving — the same mindset I now apply to digital systems and infosec.

I’m currently earning my B.S. in Cybersecurity and Information Assurance at WGU, and building out professional lab environments and documentation to prove what I can do.

Why Cybersecurity Now?

This career shift isn’t random — it’s the next evolution. I’ve secured high-risk physical systems for decades. Now, I’m applying that same energy to protecting digital infrastructure.

Through hands-on labs, certifications, and academic work, I’m developing the tools to contribute in SOC operations, automation, and threat defense.
